About us:

Ogbunike Caves is a collection of caves linked by tunnels. It is a tourist attraction and one of the UNESCO world natural heritage sites in Oyi Local Government Area, Anambra Nigeria. Of particular interests are

  • The tunnels: tunnels leading to different
  • Ogbunike reptiles: a big python (ogbunike diety), harmless crocodiles and tortoise (believed to have lived for over a century)
  • Ogbunike waterfall and warm stream pool
  • The bat parlor: over 100K bats inhabiting one of the caves
